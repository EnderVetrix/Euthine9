Euthine 9

Story

Evicted from your home planet, the Andres Fredrich Allen must defeat the evil prince and free the worlds from his control. Trust no one! Even your best friend Timothy P. Anderson has turned against you.

A game made at the Wisdom Builders Game Jam in 2016. It is very addicting and I personally cannot put it down!

Please Notify me if you find any major bugs in this game and I will quickly fix them!

Thank you all!

Controls

Arrow Keys - Move

Q and E - Shoot (Single)

W - Shield (Drains Power)

A - Bullet Arc (Drains ALL Power)

D - Destroy Asteroids (Drains Power)
